Did You Know?

Underwater volcanoes produce over 75% of the magma on Earth.

Hydrothermal vents near underwater volcanoes support unique ecosystems.

Some underwater volcanoes can grow to form new islands!

Underwater volcanoes are formed by tectonic activity. The heat from the magma supports life forms that thrive in extreme conditions, making them a hub for scientific research.
